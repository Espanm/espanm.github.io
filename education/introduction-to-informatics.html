Introduction to Informatics

For this course only the syllabus is available.

Syllabus

  • Overview of widely used end-user software in education and office workflows; practical use-cases.
  • Producing high-quality text documents: structure, formatting standards, and editing workflow.
  • Presentation software: design basics and practical creation of slides for different audiences.
  • Spreadsheets: data types, cleaning, summarization, visualization, and interpreting relationships in data.
  • Problem solving with spreadsheets through hands-on exercises (formulas, tables, charts, basic analysis).
