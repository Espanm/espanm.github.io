Operations Research II

For this course only the syllabus is available.

Syllabus

  • Totally unimodular (TU) matrices and integrality in network/LP problems.
  • Simplex method (advanced topics) and sensitivity analysis.
  • Network flows and related optimization models.
  • Basics of integer programming.
  • Decomposition methods: column generation and Lagrangian relaxation.
  • Approximation algorithms (introductory landscape).
  • Foundations of strategic game theory; Nash equilibrium and Nash theorem (statement).
